Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 17, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Smith AI Assistant ("we", "our", or "the bot") handles information when you interact with our Facebook Messenger bot.

Information We Collect

When you interact with our bot via Facebook Messenger, we may collect:

  • Messages: The content of messages you send to the bot
  • User Identifiers: Your Facebook user ID and name (provided by Facebook)
  • Interaction Data: Timestamps and metadata about your conversations

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Respond to your messages and provide the requested assistance
  • Improve the bot's functionality and user experience
  • Maintain conversation context during active sessions

Data Storage and Retention

  • Conversations are processed in real-time and stored temporarily to maintain context
  • We do not permanently store message history beyond what is necessary for active sessions
  • Data is stored securely and is not accessible to third parties

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • With your explicit consent

Third-Party Services

This bot operates via Facebook Messenger. Facebook's own privacy policy applies to their platform and services. We recommend reviewing Facebook's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your conversation data
  • Stop using the bot at any time by blocking or ignoring it
  • Contact us with privacy concerns or data requests

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our bot is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when changes were last made. Continued use of the bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
